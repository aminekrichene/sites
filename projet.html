EFFECTIVNESS OF NECOTINE GUM


Nicotine chewing gum has been tested several times , often inconclusive. We will analyze 26 of them, the criterion of judgment being the cessation of smoking during at least 6 months. We want to estimate the log-OR of stopping treatment of patients from treated group versus a control group by comparing twho bayesian models.
